Top 20 Vietnamese Fonts for Web Design in 2026

Choosing the right typography for Vietnamese web design in 2026 requires more than just looking for pretty letters. You need full Unicode support, specifically for complex tone marks that often break standard Latin-based fonts.

Pro Tip: Always test your font with a "stress test" string like: "Thế giới này thật tuyệt vời, nhưng chữ viết phải rõ ràng!"

In this guide, we explore the top choices that balance aesthetics with technical robustness for the Vietnamese locale.

1. The Modern Classics

Fonts like Inter and Plus Jakarta Sans remain the gold standard for UI/UX due to their high x-height and excellent legibility in small sizes.

2. Personality Driven

For branding that stands out, Space Grotesk and Sora offer a tech-forward, futuristic vibe that complements the 2026 aesthetic.
