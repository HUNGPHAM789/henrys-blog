Privacy Policy

YES Content App — Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data necessary to publish content to your TikTok account: your TikTok user ID and authorization token.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to publish educational content to your authorized TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Authorization tokens are stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This app interacts with TikTok's Content Posting API. TikTok's privacy policy applies to all interactions with their platform.

5. Data Deletion

You can revoke access at any time by removing the app from your TikTok account settings. All local data can be deleted by removing the application.
